About Us

Global Content Copyright Issues

Music copyright laws vary widely across countries, creating challenges in protecting and enforcing rights. The rise of digital platforms has intensified issues like unauthorized use and piracy. Independent artists often struggle with copyright enforcement and revenue generation. Technological advancements, such as blockchain, offer potential solutions, while international efforts aim to harmonize laws and improve cross-border enforcement. Balancing consumer access with fair compensation for creators remains a key challenge.
